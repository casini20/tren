01 Who We Are

Tren ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at trenai.vercel.app and the associated trading automation service. We are the data controller responsible for your personal information collected through this service.

For any privacy-related questions, you can reach us at trenhelp@outlook.com.

02 Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account data: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Payment data: Billing details (processed securely by our payment provider — we do not store raw card numbers).
  • Communications: Messages you send us via email or support channels.
  • Brokerage credentials: Connection details required to link your NinjaTrader account, stored encrypted.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the platform.
  • Device data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers.
  • Trade logs: Records of signals generated and executed trades for performance tracking and display.
  • Cookies and tracking technologies: See Section 9 for full details.

2.3 Information From Third Parties

  • Payment processors (e.g. Stripe) may share transaction confirmation data with us.
  • If you connect via Discord OAuth, we may receive your Discord username and ID.

03 How We Use Your Information

Purpose Details
Service delivery Providing access to signals, executing automated trades on your behalf, maintaining your account.
Billing & subscriptions Processing payments, managing trials, sending receipts and renewal notifications.
Communication Sending service updates, signal alerts, important notices, and responding to support requests.
Safety & fraud prevention Detecting and preventing abuse, unauthorized access, or fraudulent activity.
Platform improvement Analysing usage patterns to improve features, fix bugs, and optimize signal performance.
Legal compliance Meeting obligations under applicable law, responding to lawful requests from authorities.

💡 We do not use your data to build advertising profiles or sell insights to third parties. Our business model is subscriptions — not your data.

04 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to provide the services you've signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests: Fraud prevention, security, and improving our service — balanced against your rights.
  • Legal obligation: Complying with applicable law (e.g. financial record-keeping requirements).
  • Consent: For optional communications such as marketing emails. You can withdraw consent at any time.

05 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party processors to help operate our service. These include:

  • Payment processing: Stripe (or similar) for handling subscriptions securely.
  • Database & infrastructure: Supabase for storing trade logs and account data.
  • Communication: Discord for signal delivery and community features.
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymised usage analytics to improve the platform.

All processors are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority — or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Tren, our users, or the public.

5.3 Business Transfers

If Tren is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data is subject to a different privacy policy.

06 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active, plus up to 90 days after deletion to enable recovery.
  • Trade logs: Retained for up to 3 years for performance auditing and legal purposes.
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations.
  • Support communications: Retained for 2 years after resolution.

After applicable retention periods, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

07 Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption of data at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL).
  • Encrypted storage of brokerage connection credentials.
  • Access controls limiting who can view personal data internally.
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure.

⚠️ No system is completely secure. While we take security seriously, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential.

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it immediately to trenhelp@outlook.com.

08 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right What it means
Access Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
Rectification Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
Erasure Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention obligations.
Restriction Request that we limit processing of your data in certain circumstances.
Portability Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Objection Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.
Withdraw consent Withdraw consent at any time where processing is consent-based.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at trenhelp@outlook.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g. the Dutch Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens or the UK ICO).
