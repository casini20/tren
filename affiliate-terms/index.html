Commission Structure

Commission rate

Approved Affiliates earn a commission of 10% of the net subscription value for each Qualifying Sale attributable to their Affiliate Code. The "net subscription value" is the amount received by Tren after any applicable payment processor fees and excluding taxes.

Recurring commissions

Commissions are recurring — you earn 10% for each month a Referred Customer remains on an active, paid subscription. Commission payments stop if a Referred Customer cancels, fails to renew, or receives a refund.

Customer discount

When a Referred Customer uses your Affiliate Code at checkout, they receive 10% off their subscription. This discount applies to the first payment and subsequent recurring payments for as long as the code remains valid.

No cap

There is no cap on the number of referrals or total commissions you can earn, subject to these Terms.

Changes to commission rates

Tren may adjust commission rates at any time by providing written notice (email) to active Affiliates at least 14 days in advance. Commissions already earned on Qualifying Sales that occurred before the change take effect will be paid at the original rate.
